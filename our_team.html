The People Behind Our Mission

Our team is composed of dedicated professionals with diverse backgrounds in humanitarian work, community development, mental health, education, and advocacy. Together, we bring decades of experience and a shared commitment to serving the most vulnerable.

We believe that the strength of our organization lies in the passion, expertise, and compassion of our team members. From our leadership to our field staff, every person plays a vital role in turning our vision into reality.

Our Leadership

Founder & Executive Director

Sarah Achil

Visionary leader with 15+ years in humanitarian work

Sarah founded Achil Devine Foundation in 2015 after witnessing the struggles of refugee communities in Kampala. With a background in international development and social work, she has dedicated her career to empowering displaced persons and survivors of violence.

Under her leadership, the organization has grown from a small grassroots initiative to a recognized NGO serving thousands of people across multiple regions.

Co-Founder & Programs Director

John Devine

Expert in sustainable development and livelihood programs

John brings over 12 years of experience in designing and implementing community development programs across East Africa. His expertise in agriculture, vocational training, and economic empowerment has been instrumental in shaping the foundation's livelihood initiatives.

John believes in creating solutions that are not only effective but also sustainable and community-owned.
