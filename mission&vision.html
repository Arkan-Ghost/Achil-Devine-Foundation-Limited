Our Vision

A future of dignity and opportunity for all

A society where refugees, displaced persons, survivors of violence, and vulnerable host communities live with dignity, equal opportunities, and sustainable livelihoods.

"We envision a world where displacement does not mean despair, where every person has the chance to rebuild their life with hope and purpose."

Our Mission

Empowering the most vulnerable

To empower refugees, asylum seekers, survivors of sexual and gender-based violence, and vulnerable host communities through protection, education, skills development, psychosocial support, and sustainable livelihood programs.

"We work alongside communities, building on their strengths and accompanying them on their journey toward self-reliance."

Our Strategic Objectives

These are the key pillars that guide our work and help us achieve our mission.

01

Protection & Safety

Ensure the protection of refugees and survivors through legal aid, safe spaces, and advocacy.

02

Education & Skills

Provide access to quality education and vocational training for youth and adults.

03

Health & Wellbeing

Promote physical and mental health through community health programs and psychosocial support.

04

Economic Empowerment

Create sustainable livelihood opportunities through skills training and access to capital.

05

Community Integration

Foster social cohesion between refugees and host communities.

06

Advocacy & Rights

Advocate for the rights of displaced persons and influence policies.

How We Achieve Our Mission

Our approach combines direct service delivery with community participation and advocacy.

1

Listen & Understand

We begin by listening to communities to understand their needs and aspirations.

2

Co-Design Solutions

We work with communities to design programs that are contextually appropriate.

3

Implement Together

We implement programs in partnership with local actors and community members.

4

Monitor & Learn

We continuously monitor our impact and learn from both successes and challenges.

5

Advocate for Change

We use evidence to advocate for policy changes at local and national levels.

6

Scale & Sustain

We scale successful interventions and ensure their long-term sustainability.
