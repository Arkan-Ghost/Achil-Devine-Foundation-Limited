About Achil Devine Foundation Limited

Achil Devine Foundation Limited is a humanitarian and community development organization dedicated to supporting refugees, asylum seekers, survivors of gender-based violence, and vulnerable host communities.

Through education, livelihood programs, health services, and advocacy, we aim to create opportunities that promote dignity, self-reliance, and sustainable development.

Our Impact

1,000+ people supported
500 youth trained
300 women empowered
10 communities reached

Our Approach

We adopt a holistic and participatory approach for sustainable and transformative impact.

Participatory

We actively involve communities in the design and implementation of our programs.

  • Community consultations
  • Collective decision-making
  • Local leadership

Holistic

We address challenges from all angles: social, economic, health, and psychological.

  • Physical and mental health
  • Economic empowerment
  • Psychosocial support

Sustainable

We build solutions that last beyond our immediate intervention.

  • Capacity building
  • Local partnerships
  • Monitoring & evaluation

Inclusive

We reach the most vulnerable, including persons with disabilities and survivors of violence.

Innovative

We use creative and technological solutions to maximize our impact.
