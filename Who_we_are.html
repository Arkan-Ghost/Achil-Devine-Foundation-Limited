About Achil Devine Foundation Limited

Achil Devine Foundation Limited is a humanitarian and community development organization dedicated to supporting refugees, asylum seekers, survivors of gender-based violence, and vulnerable host communities.

Founded in 2015, we have grown from a small grassroots initiative to a recognized organization working across multiple regions. Our name represents the fusion of two families united by a common vision: "Achil" symbolizing strength and resilience in local languages, and "Devine" representing our belief in the divine dignity of every human being.

We believe that every person, regardless of their circumstances, deserves to live with dignity, access opportunities, and build a sustainable future for themselves and their families.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to expanding impact, here are the key milestones in our organization's history.

2022

Foundation established by Achil Maluma to support 50 refugee families in Kampala.

2023

Expanded programs to include mental health support and vocational training for youth.

2024

Reached 500+ beneficiaries and opened second office in Northern Uganda.

2025

Launched agriculture programs and formed partnerships with 5 international NGOs.

2026

Celebrated supporting over 1,000 individuals across 10 communities.

2024

Expanding to new regions with innovative livelihood and health programs.

What Guides Us

Our work is built on these foundational principles that shape every program we implement.

People-Centered

We place the needs and aspirations of communities at the center of everything we do.

Collaborative

We work hand-in-hand with communities, partners, and local authorities.

Sustainable

We build solutions that last beyond our direct intervention.

Evidence-Based

We use data and learning to continuously improve our programs.

Compassionate

We approach our work with empathy, respect, and kindness.

Accountable

We are transparent and responsible to our beneficiaries and donors.
