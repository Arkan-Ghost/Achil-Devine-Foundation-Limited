About the Organization

Achil Devine Foundation Limited is committed to promoting dignity, inclusion, and sustainable livelihoods for refugees, displaced persons, survivors of violence, and vulnerable host communities.

Founded with a vision to create lasting change, we work tirelessly to ensure that the most vulnerable members of society have access to the resources, support, and opportunities they need to rebuild their lives with dignity.

Our approach is rooted in community participation and local leadership, ensuring that our interventions are culturally appropriate, context-specific, and truly owned by the communities we serve.

Our Vision

A society where refugees, displaced persons, survivors of violence, and vulnerable host communities live with dignity, equal opportunities, and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Mission

To empower refugees, asylum seekers, survivors of sexual and gender-based violence, and vulnerable host communities through protection, education, skills development, psychosocial support, and sustainable livelihood programs.

Our Core Values

These values guide everything we do, from program design to implementation and evaluation.

Human dignity and respect

We treat every person with inherent dignity and respect, recognizing their strengths and capabilities.

Equity and inclusion

We ensure that all individuals, regardless of their background, have equal access to opportunities and resources.

Transparency and accountability

We operate with openness and hold ourselves accountable to our beneficiaries, partners, and donors.

Community participation

We actively involve communities in decision-making processes that affect their lives.

Empowerment and self-reliance

We build capacities and create opportunities for individuals and communities to become self-sufficient.

Protection of human rights

We advocate for and protect the fundamental rights of all people, especially the most vulnerable.

Sustainable development

We promote solutions that create lasting change for refugees and host communities alike.
